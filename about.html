About Us

A Legacy of Taste, Built on Trust

Every successful journey begins with a dream. Ours began with a simple family tradition of grinding spices for our customers.

What started as a small family business gradually evolved into Chirlekar Masala, driven by a vision to preserve the authentic taste of Maharashtrian cuisine while maintaining the highest standards of quality and purity.

Our founder chose to leave a corporate career after completing Engineering and an MBA, returning to the family's roots with one goal—to build a trusted spice brand that every household could rely on.

Today, every pack of Chirlekar Masala is crafted using carefully selected spices, traditional recipes, and modern hygienic processing.

Our Vision

To become one of Maharashtra's most trusted spice brands by delivering authentic taste, superior quality, and complete customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To preserve traditional recipes while embracing modern manufacturing practices, ensuring every family enjoys fresh, pure, and flavorful spices.

Our Values

Authentic Taste Premium Quality Purity & Hygiene Customer Trust Innovation with Tradition
